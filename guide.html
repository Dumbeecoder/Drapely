Everything you need to know to get stunning AI fashion photos, videos and bulk shoots in minutes.

AI Photoshoot
Upload a suit photo → choose model, scene & pose → generate professional photos in seconds
1
Upload your suit photo
Take a clear photo of your garment laid flat or on a hanger. Make sure the full outfit is visible with good lighting. JPG or PNG works best.
2
Choose garment type
Select Salwar Suit (Kurta, Anarkali, Palazzo) or Saree (Silk, Chiffon, Cotton). This helps the AI understand how to drape the outfit correctly.
3
Pick a model
Choose from 6 Indian models — Priya, Anjali, Kavya, Simran, Meera, Naina. Or upload your own model photo for a custom look.
4
Choose scene & pose
Pick from 12 realistic photoshoot backgrounds (Marble Corridor, Garden, Palace, etc.) or write your own custom scene. Select a pose for the model.
5
Select image count & generate
Choose 1, 2 or 4 images. Each image = 1 credit (₹50). Click Generate and your photos will be ready in 30–60 seconds.
6
Download & share
Download HD photos directly. All photos are saved in My Gallery. You can also turn any photo into an AI fashion video.
📸 What makes the best suit photos
☀️
Good lightingNatural light or bright indoor light. Avoid dark or shadowy photos.
👗
Full outfit visibleMake sure the entire garment — top, bottom and dupatta — is in frame.
🧹
Clean backgroundWhite or plain background works best. Avoid cluttered backgrounds.
📐
Flat lay or hangerLay the suit flat on a bed/table or hang it up. Both work great.
🎨
Show embroidery clearlyCapture the embroidery, zari work and details — the AI will replicate them.
📱
High resolutionUse your phone camera normally. Avoid blurry or zoomed-out photos.
✅ DO
Upload the full suit including dupatta
Use bright, even lighting
Keep suit wrinkle-free if possible
Use a plain white/light background
Show the front of the garment clearly
❌ DON'T
Upload blurry or dark photos
Crop out parts of the outfit
Use heavily patterned backgrounds
Upload photos of models wearing suits
Upload very small or low-res images
How many credits does one photoshoot cost? ▼
1 image = 1 credit (₹50). You can generate 1, 2 or 4 images per photoshoot. So a 4-image shoot costs 4 credits (₹200).
Can I use my own background? ▼
Yes! In Step 3 click "Upload background" to upload any image. The AI will remove the generated background and place the model on your image. You can also download free backgrounds from the Backgrounds page.
What if the result doesn't look right? ▼
Try uploading a clearer photo of the garment with better lighting. Also try a different model or scene. The quality of the input photo greatly affects the output.
Where are my generated photos saved? ▼
All photos are automatically saved to My Gallery. You can view, download and share them anytime.

AI Fashion Video
Upload a suit photo or use a generated photo → describe movement → get a 5s or 10s fashion video
1
Upload a photo
Upload a suit photo directly, or go to My Gallery and click "Make Video" on any generated photo. Using a Drapely-generated photo gives better results.
2
Describe what the model should do
Type or select what movement you want — walking, twirling, standing confidently, showing dupatta, doing a full turn. Be specific for best results.
3
Choose video length
5 seconds = 2 credits (₹100). 10 seconds = 4 credits (₹200). Longer videos show more movement and are better for social media.
4
Generate & download
Click Generate. Videos take 2–3 minutes to process. Once ready, download your video or share it directly on WhatsApp.
🎬 Tips for the best videos
🖼️
Use Drapely photosVideos made from Drapely-generated photos look much smoother and more realistic.
💃
Be specific about movement"Model walking gracefully showing full outfit" works better than just "walking".
🌀
Twirls show dupatta bestFor suits with flowing dupatta, twirling or arms-open poses show the fabric beautifully.
⏱️
10s for social media10-second videos are better for Instagram Reels and WhatsApp Status.
✅ Best movements to request
Walking gracefully, showing full outfit
Twirling, dupatta flowing beautifully
Standing confidently, posing for camera
Slow 360 degree turn showing full outfit
Walking towards camera confidently
⚠️ Avoid these
Very complex multi-step movements
Vague prompts like just "move"
Requesting specific facial expressions
Uploading very dark or blurry photos
Expecting exact scene backgrounds in video
How long does video generation take? ▼
Videos take 2–3 minutes to process. Don't close the page while waiting. Once done, the video appears automatically and is saved to My Gallery.
Can I use any photo for video? ▼
Yes, you can upload any suit photo. But videos made from Drapely AI-generated photos look significantly better since the model and outfit are already optimized.
Where do my videos get saved? ▼
All videos are saved to My Gallery under the Videos tab. You can download them or share directly to WhatsApp.

Bulk Studio
Upload up to 20 suit photos at once → generate all → download as ZIP
1
Upload multiple suit photos
Select up to 20 suit photos at once. Each photo = 1 credit (₹50). Make sure you have enough credits before starting. All photos should be clear with good lighting.
2
Choose settings for all
Select garment type, scene/background, model and pose. These settings apply to all photos in the batch — choose what looks best for your collection.
3
Generate all at once
Click Generate All. Each suit is processed one by one. You can see progress in real time as each photo is generated. Takes 1–2 minutes per photo.
4
Download as ZIP
Once all photos are done, click Export ZIP to download all photos in one file. All photos are also saved to My Gallery automatically.
⚡ Tips for bulk shoots
🗂️
Batch by styleGroup similar suits together (all salwar suits in one batch, sarees in another) for consistent results.
💳
Check credits firstMake sure you have enough credits before starting. 20 photos = 20 credits.
📸
Consistent photo styleTake all garment photos the same way — same lighting, same angle — for a cohesive catalog.
🌅
Choose one scenePick a single background scene that works for your whole collection (White Studio works for all).
✅ Bulk works best for
Catalog shoots for online stores
Festival collection launches
WhatsApp catalogue for customers
Instagram product grid photos
Saree or suit set collections
⚠️ Keep in mind
Don't close the page during generation
All photos use the same model & scene
Max 20 photos per batch
Credits are deducted per photo generated
Poor quality photos = poor quality results
Can I choose different models for each photo in bulk? ▼
Not currently — bulk shoots use one model for the entire batch. For different models, use the regular photoshoot which lets you pick per photo.
What if some photos in the batch fail? ▼
Failed photos are skipped and only successful ones are included in the ZIP download. Credits are only deducted for successful generations.
How long does a 20-photo bulk shoot take? ▼
Each photo takes about 1–2 minutes, so a 20-photo batch can take 20–40 minutes. Keep the page open during generation.
